Recipe

Let's prepare the loved-by-everyone Starbucks Iced Caramel Macchiato at home! I'll share the tips and the secrets on how to easily make this delicious treat.

Slightly sweetened milk on the bottom, ice in the middle, bittersweet espresso on top and rich caramel sauce all over the cup… Isn’t this what you are craving this summer? Get ready to make your very own Iced Caramel Macchiato!

  • Course: Drinks
  • Cuisine: American
  • Servings: 2 12 oz cups
  • Calories: 110 kcal

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp vanilla syrup.
  • 1 1/2 cup milk.
    • Starbucks usually use 2% milk, but you can choose any milk to your liking. If you use vanilla soy milk, you may skip the vanilla syrup altogether.
  • 2 oz espresso
  • Caramel sauce to drizzle
    • Ghirardelli Caramel Sauce is my recommendation!
  • Ice

Instructions

  1. Add vanilla syrup to the cup.
    • 2 tsp for 12oz (small sized) cup,
    • 3 tsp for 16oz (medium sized) cup,
    • 4 tsp for 20oz (large sized) cup,
    • Of course, you can adjust the sweetness to your liking.
  2. Add milk over the syrup (approx. halfway).
  3. Add ice, reserving room for espresso.
  4. Add espresso.
    • 1, 2, or 3oz for small, medium, or large sized cup respectively.
  5. Drizzle with caramel sauce on top and enjoy!

Hope you’ll enjoy the deliciousness of this Iced Caramel Macchiato as much as I did!
